Introduction

Maira Khan is the owner of Black Cats Arts. She's a Pakistani-Irish based artist who loves to express her gifted creativity in the form of bewildering illustrations. She is an accomplished Calligraphist, a skilled Oil painter and much more.

My Story

Hi! I am Maira Khan. A dentist by profession and an artist by passion. I am currently residing in Ireland, Tralee County Kerry. I explored this hidden talent in me during my college tenure by participating in different art competitions and to my surprise winning them. This winning is just not my thing, for me arts is nourishing my soul and for me my artwork is priceless and i really want that my original artwork resides with those people who really link to it emotionally and aesthetically. Its original artwork so there is no recreation of one painting which has been already out. I also take orders for customized paintings and i am interested in interior designing and decorating walls of home's and cafe's
