Introduction

Maira Khan is the owner of Black Cats Arts. She's a Pakistani-Irish based artist who loves to express her gifted creativity in the form of bewildering illustrations.

My Story

Hi I am Maira Khan owner of Black Cat Arts. I am a dentist by profession and an Artist by passion. I am currently residing in Ireland ,Tullamore Co.Offaly. I explored this hidden talent in me during my college tenure and in pursuit of my passion I started to work in different art mediums which has now led me to a point where I work as an artist. For me, art is nourishing my soul and I really enjoy all my creation. Every piece is very dear to me and I want that my original artwork resides with those people who really link to it emotionally and aesthetically. Enjoy my Gallery.

Hope to beautify your home soon! 💙
